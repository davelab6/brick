Why Brick?

  • Beautiful

    We never remove kerning or hinting data from fonts, and we're always looking for ways to make webfonts look prettier in any browser.

  • Fast

    Brick is powered by Fastly's global all-SSD CDN, and backed by a (also all-SSD!) Linode server, so fonts are delivered speedily no matter where in the world your users are.

  • Open source

    Best of all, Brick is open source, meaning that in addition to being free, it's also constantly improving with contributions from the community.
