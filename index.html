Webfonts that
actually look good.

Serve better webfonts faster with Brick. With a large catalog of high quality, open source fonts, you can find a style for any situation.

Browse fonts →

Fonts render the way they were meant to be seen.

We don't modify the font data in any way, so the fonts are rendered beautifully in the browser. Brick supports kerning, ligatures, and full character sets, and we always try to keep our fonts up-to-date, so you always have the latest and greatest fonts and features.

Loading speeds are just as important as font quality.

Fonts are served through Fastly's industry-leading CDN and supported by reliable backend from Linode, providing the fastest loading speeds possible. We're also super proud that Brick runs on 100% SSD infrastructure.

Forever free & open source.

Brick is free open source, so not only can you use the service with little to no restrictions, but you can contribute to the project as well! Brick is maintained by a small community of contributors, all of whom volunteer their time to make the project better.
